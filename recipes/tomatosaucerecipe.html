Tomato Sauce Recipe

Chunky, delicious tomato sauce to be used for so many dishes!

This tomato sauce is a family classic and has been passed down for generations.

You can use this recipe for chicken parmesan or simply used with some toasted bread!

Ingredients

  • 4 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
  • 2 large cans of crushed tomato
  • 1 large can of tomato puree
  • 1 large whole tomato
  • 1 small can of tomato paste
  • 1 whole large onion
  • 6 whole cloves of fresh garlic
  • 2 Tbsp of red pepper flakes
  • Parsley and Oregano to taste
  • 5 sprigs of fresh basil
Directions
  1. Add EVOO to bottom of pot
  2. Slice onion into thin strips and add to pot on medium heat
  3. Smash your garlic and add to pot (pressing down with palm of hand)
  4. Add your crushed red pepper, parsley, oregano, and salt & pepper
  5. Turn up your temperature to medium-high heat and let ingredients simmer for 2 minutes
  6. Once oil and ingredients are simmering for 2 minutes then add your tomato paste and stir.
  7. Stir in your crushed tomato, tomato puree, and whole tomato
  8. Crush whole tomato with spoon or crushing utensil
  9. Add basil sprigs on top of sauce
  10. Cover and simmer on low-medium heat for 3-5 hours stirring every 30 minutes
